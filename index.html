Find the IA

> Rules

As a dedicated detective, it's your mission to uncover the truth behind a recent crime. You'll interrogate each suspect about their activities on the night of the incident and have the option to verify their alibis by contacting a close friend. However, time is of the essence, and you're limited to asking only five questions. After gathering your information, you'll need to make a crucial decision, but be cautious, this is your one and only chance to solve the case!
